WELCOME TO SARASWATI SISHU VIDYA MANDIR, NILADRI VIHAR

Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir is a unique institution based on Indian tradition and culture. In all over India there are approximately 25,000 institutions running under the guidance of Vidya Bharati. "Shiksha Vikash Samiti, Odisha" is a registered organization affiliated with Vidya Bharati. Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir, Niladri Vihar, Bhubaneswar is one of the best institutions among all 700 institutions running under Siksha Vikash Samiti. It was established on the day of Rathayatra, 1993, and working towards the spiritual, educational development and character building of child.
The school is housed in a magnificent building with a large playground and well-equipped laboratories, library, big auditorium, dedicated batika, and computer lab. The school has a strength of about 1900 students and 76 teachers and about 22 non-teaching staff. It is also equipped with advanced audio-visual aids. The school follows the courses and syllabus of Board of Secondary Education, Odisha and runs classes from Nursery to class-X with Odia as a medium of instruction.
