Welcome to our School Library

Purpose:

The school library serves as a vital resource center to support academic excellence, foster a love for reading, and promote lifelong learning among students and faculty members. It provides access to a diverse collection of books, periodicals, digital resources, and educational materials to enrich the learning experience.

Library Rules:

  1. Respect the library staff and fellow library users.
  2. Handle library materials with care and return them on time.
  3. Keep noise levels to a minimum to maintain a conducive environment for study and reading.
  4. Use electronic devices responsibly and adhere to the school's acceptable use policy.
  5. Food and drinks are not permitted in the library to preserve the cleanliness of the facilities and protect library materials.
  6. Follow all borrowing and return procedures as outlined by the library staff.
